# CarPark Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-28

This Privacy Policy explains how CarPark collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the CarPark iPhone application and its connected backend services.

CarPark is an iPhone-first vehicle catalog and lead request application for car dealerships.

## 1. Information We Collect

CarPark does not require customers to create an account.

When you browse the vehicle catalog, save cars locally, or view vehicle details, we do not require your name, email address, password, payment card, or customer profile.

When you submit a callback or inquiry request, CarPark may collect the information you choose to provide:

- your name;
- your phone number;
- your email address, if provided;
- your message, if provided;
- the vehicle or listing you are interested in;
- related dealership context needed to process the request.

CarPark may also process basic technical information required to operate the service, such as:

- request time;
- IP address;
- server logs;
- device/network request metadata;
- error and security logs.

This technical information is used only to operate, protect, debug, and maintain the service.

## 2. Information Stored Locally On Your Device

CarPark may store saved vehicle IDs locally on your device so you can keep a shortlist of cars.

Saved cars are stored locally on your device and are not synced to a customer account in the current version.

If you delete the app or clear local app data, saved cars may be removed.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- show vehicle listings and vehicle details;
- process callback or inquiry requests;
- allow dealership staff to respond to customer inquiries;
- protect public forms from spam and abuse;
- maintain, debug, and improve service reliability;
- keep operational and security records where needed.

We do not sell personal information.

We do not use customer data for third-party advertising tracking.

## 4. Contact Actions And Third-Party Apps

CarPark may allow you to contact a dealership through phone, WhatsApp, Telegram, or Viber actions.

When you choose to open one of these services, you leave CarPark and interact with the selected third-party app or service. Your use of those services is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

## 5. Data Sharing

CarPark may share inquiry information with the dealership or authorized staff responsible for processing your request.

CarPark may also use infrastructure providers for hosting, database storage, media storage, backups, security, and service operation.

We do not sell personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

## 6. Data Retention

Customer inquiry data may be retained as part of dealership sales history, support, operational records, and legal or business obligations.

Saved cars stored locally on your device remain on the device until you remove them, delete the app, or clear local app data.

Operational logs and backups may be retained for security, debugging, backup, and restore purposes.

You may contact us to request review, correction, or deletion of your personal inquiry data where applicable.

## 7. Security

CarPark uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including:

- HTTPS transport where deployed;
- backend validation of public requests;
- phone number validation and normalization;
- rate limits for public lead submission;
- staff authentication;
- environment-based secret configuration;
- backup and restore procedures.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect the service using practical security controls.

## 8. Children

CarPark is not intended for children under 13. The service is designed for vehicle browsing and dealership inquiries.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. No Payments In The Current Version

The current CarPark application does not process customer payments inside the app.

If payments, deposits, subscriptions, or paid listing placement are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are released.

## 10. Future Features

CarPark may later add features such as customer profiles, server-synced saved cars, notifications, paid listing placement, website inventory import, or dealership subscriptions.

If future features change how personal data is collected or used, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are made publicly available.

## 11. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
