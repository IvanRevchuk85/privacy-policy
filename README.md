This Privacy Policy explains how the apps Timer for Work and TimeKit handle information.

Apps Covered
Timer for Work (macOS, iPadOS)

Summary
We do not collect, store, or share personal data.
The apps work without creating accounts.
Any settings or timer configurations are stored locally on your device.
Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information or any user-identifiable data.

We do not run our own servers to receive your data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The apps may store the following locally to provide core functionality:

Timer for Work
Workday plan configuration (work/break/lunch segments)
App preferences (appearance, notifications settings, schedule settings)
Local timer state needed for the current session
TimeKit
Presets (saved interval/Tabata configurations)
App preferences (sounds/haptics, UI options)
Local timer/stopwatch state needed for the current session

You can remove this local data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

We do not receive your payment details (such as card numbers). Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple’s policies.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
Sharing of Data

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data because we do not collect it.

Children’s Privacy

The apps do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.
